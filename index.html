My CV
I am working in bioinformatics research.

I am a PhD candidate in bioinformatics research with software development and system administration background. The aims of my PhD research are to identify and characterize genome diversity in Triticum aestivum (bread wheat).

My research provides approaches to understand the effect of sequence variation on the form and function of wheat, growth and development and response to environment. My research interests focus on genome informatics,  including the second generation sequence characterisation of complex plant and animal genomes, genome annotation, diversity analysis and data visualisation, with a focus on wheat crops.
RECENT PROJECT

I am working in the discovery of large numbers of genomic SNPs across 16 diverse Australian bread wheat varieties.
